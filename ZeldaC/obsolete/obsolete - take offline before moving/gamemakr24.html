About GameMakr24:

I'm a college student who hopes to someday create incredibly fun video games. Currently, I'm majoring in computer science and minoring in creative writing. I've been married for almost 4 years to a beautiful red-head, but we don't have any kids yet. Our cats keep us busy enough as it is...

My favorite games are Action/Adventure, RPGs, and puzzle games. I've got a NES, SNES, Genesis, Color Gameboy, N64 and a Playstation, and almost no time to play any of them. Outside the scope of gaming, I like anime, reading, running long distance, and programming, among other things.

Here at Challenge Games, my current goal is to complete my "How to Hack Zelda" guide. Now that I've finished Zelda Challenge: Outlands (a complete edit of The Legend of Zelda), I'm planning to make Zelda 3 my next project. More on this to come, as information develops.
